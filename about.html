About Us

At MIS (Maintenance & Installation Services Ltd), we are dedicated to protecting what matters most — your people, property, and peace of mind.

With years of hands-on experience in the fire safety, security, and data infrastructure industry, we specialize in the installation, servicing, and maintenance of fire detection systems, intruder alarms, CCTV, access control, and structured data cabling. Whether it’s a small business, a large commercial site, or a residential property, we deliver tailored solutions built to last.

We pride ourselves on reliability, technical excellence, and an unwavering commitment to safety. Every system we install is installed to protect, and every visit we make is maintained to last.

Let MIS be your trusted partner in fire, security, and data — because your safety is our business.
